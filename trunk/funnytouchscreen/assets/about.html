How to play

"Funny Touch Screen" is a free set of two simple games with four levels of difficulty. Games are intended for babies and preschool children.

In the first game user clicks on buttons and they disappear. When all buttons are gone, next round (with more buttons) is started. Easiest level allows user to click on any button, the most difficult level requires to click in the order specified by numbers. There are four different levels for different age of your child.

In the second game user picks two buttons of the same kind and then they disappear. Game is also available in four difficulty levels. Pairs of buttons have same color, same number, etc.

Personalization

Feel free to add your own photos and music. Connect your phone to the computer, go to the "funny-touch-screen" folder on the SD card and add as many images or music files you want. Make sure this files end with ".jpg" or ".mp3", other formats will be ignored.
